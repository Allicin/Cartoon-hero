HIRO HAMADA

About Hiro

Hiro Hamada is a genius kid that is loves making robots and attending them to duels. He is a prodigy but doesn't know where he belong. With his friends and family, he learnt his passion and continued on the legacy of his late brother. He now continues to save San Fransokyo, his city, together with his gang of nerds and Baymax
